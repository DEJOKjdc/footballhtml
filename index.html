HOW TO PLAY Football

Origins of Football

The game of football, also known as soccer in some countries, has ancient roots. It can be traced back to China’s Han Dynasty (206 BC – 220 AD), where a game called Cuju was played with a leather ball.

Modern Football

The modern version of football began to take shape in 19th century England. Different schools and clubs played using their own rules, which led to the need for a standard code.

The Formation of The FA

In 1863, the Football Association (FA) was established in England. This led to a uniform set of rules, separating it from rugby.

Global Spread

Football’s popularity spread worldwide through colonial influence, trade, and migration. By the early 20th century, it was being played on every continent.

FIFA and the World Cup

FIFA (Fédération Internationale de Football Association) was founded in 1904. The first FIFA World Cup was held in 1930 in Uruguay.

Famous Moments in Football

  1. 1958 – Pelé's debut World Cup win with Brazil
  2. 1986 – Maradona's "Hand of God" and "Goal of the Century"
  3. 2010 – Spain wins its first World Cup
